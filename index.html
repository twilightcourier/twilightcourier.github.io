I am a visual accompanist, much of my work is glitchy, dark, and minimal. I usually do visuals for harder styles of music such as (Hardcore, Hardstyle, Gabber, J-core, Happy Hardcore, Speedcore, Electro, DnB).

When planning visuals for an event, I enjoy collaborating with event organizers to ensure that visual design supports the concept and energy of the event.

I have mixed visuals at Club Kaiku, which is one of the most established nightclubs in Helsinki, frequently hosting international DJs. I was selected to participate in the national VJ competition in the city of Tampere. I have played at many underground parties, festivals, and events.
